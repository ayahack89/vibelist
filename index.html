Welcome

A personal place for great music — simple, calm, meaningful.

Vibelist is a lightweight, frontend-first music playground for curating short previews and personal playlists. Browse, save, and enjoy short preview clips — designed for clarity and ease, not affiliated with commercial streaming services.

Explore the App Learn more
Curated playlists • short previews (~30s) • demo & inspiration
Note: Vibelist is a personal, frontend-only demo built for exploration and inspiration. It uses public music APIs to fetch metadata and short preview clips (≈30 seconds). For full-length streaming or commercial use, please use licensed services such as Spotify or Apple Music. This project is Spotify-inspired and intended for personal / educational use only.
